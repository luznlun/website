Talk Tummee Mobile App

Second place winner team. In Spring 2015, a Intel's innovative contest, SJSU/ Intel Rapid Prototyping Challenge - RPC v2.0, gave me an opportunity to be creative and have fun while making a realistic prototype. By using the Intel Edison microcontroller, my team and I successfully created a solution to solve the problem while feeding newborn children. Our product makes the parents live easier by automating the baby feeding record process. Also the product is suggested to use cloud computing to respond live feedback for baby feeding advice and supports. In this team, I was the software developer, and developed a Android App to support the device and live demo. SJSU Department of Engineering wrote us an article. It was my honor to praticiate this event.

  • Client: Intel
  • Date: March 2015 to May 2015
  • Service: Software innovation
